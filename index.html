How It Works

1

Touch anywhere

Place a finger anywhere on the video — no target zone required.

2

Draw a circle

Move your finger in a circular path directly over the active video.

3

Direction = control

Clockwise advances. Counterclockwise rewinds. Natural and instant.

4

Speed = rate

Gesture speed maps to scrub speed — from frame-by-frame to rapid scan.

5

Lift to resume

Remove your finger and playback resumes from the new position.

Digi-Jog vs. Progress Bar

Traditional Progress Bar

  • Fixed to screen bottom edge
  • Requires two hands on large devices
  • Imprecise on long videos
  • Hidden during playback
  • No speed feedback loop

Digi-Jog

  • Works anywhere on screen
  • Single thumb, one hand
  • Frame-by-frame to fast-forward
  • Works on active video
  • Gesture speed = scrub speed
